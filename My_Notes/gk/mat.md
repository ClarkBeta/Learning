## 概率统计
### 二项分布
$X \sim \text{B}(n, p)$

最可能成功次数 
$$
k=\left\{\begin{matrix} 
\left [ \left ( n+1 \right )*p  \right ] ,\left [ \left ( n+1 \right )*p  \right ] \notin \mathrm {z}\\   
\left [ \left ( n+1 \right )*p  \right ] \text{或} \left [ \left ( n+1 \right )*p  -1\right ],\left [ \left ( n+1 \right )*p  \right ] \in \mathrm {z}     
\end{matrix}\right. 
$$
